About Us

It all started with an idea. Whilst in lockdown in 2020, a large group of gamers started to talk and play together online, we did this to socialize as restrictions were in place. Hours upon hours of group games were played and the number of friends became larger and larger. With every new person joining, it was pretty much guaranteed that they would bring someone extra along. When the group hit 200 gamers all in one location, a small part of them (the originals) decided it would be a good idea to possibly show others what we were doing during our gaming time. A name was brought forward and everyone jumped at the chance to be able to contribute. We started recording videos and streaming content for others to see, we have made a lot of friends overseas and have blossomed friendships that will last a lifetime. Now TheHolyRagers has become a company which distributes content online. We are glad to become what we are and hope that others will come and enjoy the fun. There is no bullying within the Ragers, no person is looked down upon and everyone is made to feel safe. We hope you enjoy being here with us!!
